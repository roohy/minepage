Projects

I-LASH

I-Lash is my latest project. It's an ultra-rapid tool for finding Identity By Descent tracts shared between people in a large scale DNA pool. It's results were presented at the 2017 ASHG meeting.
I-Lash will be available for download soon. And our results will be published along with it as well.

Extreme Pseudo-Sampling

Extreme Pseudo-Sampling is a new pipeline that uses Variational Autoencoders to find better ranking of genes in cancer survival analysis. This was my Deep Learning class project that got exciting!!! And will be submitted to be reviewed soon.

Personal Tracking System

In a team of 3, we designed and implemented a personal tracking system for cars and other valuable objects. This system is still maintained and has been under use for the past 4 years.
